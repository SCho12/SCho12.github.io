Section 3

The greatest challenge related to my consumption of video games and more specifically PC gaming is the access to computer parts in today's current technological landscape. Due to the rise of artificial intelligence, it is becoming much more difficult to acquire a graphics card needed to play games on your PC. Rather than competing with other customers, consumers are now competing with bots and scalpers whose only wish is to resell these components at a much higher value. In addition, other manufacturers of graphics cards are beginning to charge outrageous prices well above MSRP to consumers for their particular brand.
